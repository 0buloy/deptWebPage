Executive Summary

The general purpose of the study was to develop a system called KATIPUNAN NG KABATAAN INFORMATION SYSTEM. This system provided a modern solution to the lack of a consolidated and updated database that contains relevant information about the youth of Barangay Sampiruhan, Calamba City.

According to Bidani (n.d.), implementing Barangay Management Information Systems (BMIS) assisted in identifying barangay requirements, which is critical for planning nutrition and development, implementing initiatives, monitoring progress, and assessing them.

"BaristaffLog: An Employee Management System for Kafe Kultura" was the researchers add developers capstone project. This program would help Kafe Kultura's owner and staff organize their personel efficiently. It would let shop manager record, track and manage personnel schedules.
